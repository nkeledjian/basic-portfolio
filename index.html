About Me

Hello World! Web/Software Developer in training here. During my teens, I built computers for friends and family, gaining experience with hardware/software installation, and some basic IT support. During high school, I briefly experimented with Java, 3D modelling for video games, and was big into gaming and creating electronic music. Through certain personal experiences, my path took me toward the field of psychology, taking on an interest for cognition and related topics.

Fast foward to the present, after taking a leap of faith training as a therapist, I realized my interests leaned more toward studying cognition. Post graduate school, I have come full circle and am diving head first into tech!
